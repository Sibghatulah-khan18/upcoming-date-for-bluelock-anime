📖 Synopsis

Blue Lock is an intense sports anime following Isagi Yoichi, a promising young soccer player with an unconventional vision for the game. Recruited into the secretive Blue Lock facility, a specialized training program designed to develop the world's most powerful striker, Isagi competes against Japan's top young talents in a high-stakes tournament.


With unique abilities, strategic gameplay, and thrilling matches, Blue Lock explores themes of ambition, rivalry, and the pursuit of excellence. Each character brings their own weapons and strategies to the field, creating unforgettable soccer showdowns that redefine the sport itself.

👥 Main Characters
